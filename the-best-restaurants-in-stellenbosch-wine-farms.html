The best restaurants in Stellenbosch wine farms

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Nothing surpasses the joy that good wine accompanying a delicious meal provides. The two complement each other and are the best form of luxury especially when the ambience is perfect. If you need the ultimate dining experience accompanied by fine fermented grape juice, you do not need to scratch your head. The top restaurants in the Stellenbosch wine farms will meet your needs. Start planning for this beautiful experience right away!

South Africa takes pride in possessing some of the best wine farms globally. While the Cape has numerous farms and wineries, the ultimate experience can only be witnessed at the Stellenbosch wine farms that have the best restaurants. These farms grow their grapes and use the latest technologies and processes to produce the best-tasting and highest-quality wine. If you are yet to visit one of these top farms, you should do so soon. The experience will be worth your money and time!

Visiting a restaurant on a grape farm offers more than a meal and fermented grape juice. There are other fun activities that you and your loved ones can enjoy. Each farm has specific unique attributes that offer a competitive edge over others in the same region. It is, therefore, essential to visit different farms often to get an unmatched experience. According to insideguide.co.za the best restaurants in Stellenbosch wine farms are;

1. Rust En Vrede Restaurant located at the Rust En Vrede Wine Estate

At the Rust En Vrede Restaurant, every visitor can expect nothing but perfection. Its environs are warm and welcoming, and there are panoramic views of one of the top Somerset West wine farms. The staff is friendly and always willing to meet your needs. They welcome you on arrival and offer exemplary service throughout the meal. The restaurant serves different cuisines that are prepared by seasoned chefs. In 2016, Rust En Vrede Restaurant was listed among the top 100 restaurants globally by Elite Traveler.

2. Jordan Restaurant located at the Jordan Wine Estate

The scenic views as you enjoy your fermented grape juice and food are amazing. The Jordan Restaurant is one of the top 10 wine farms in Stellenbosch that is known for tasty dishes. It is best known for its wide array of cheeses that go well with the wine. The establishment features a bakery as well and is the best place for lazy lunches, breakfast and wine tasting in Stellenbosch.

3. The Table Restaurant located at the De Meye Wine Farm

This restaurant in a Stellenbosch wine farm has the best views of the Stellenbosch vineyards that offer a fantasy-like feeling. Their food is grown on the farm. The wines that they serve are pressed at the in-house winery that is ranked high among the Stellenbosch wineries. The hosts are always available to ensure that you get the best service and experience that will make you visit the place over and over.

4. The Manor Kitchen located at the Lanzerac Wine Estate

Like a boomerang, the Manor Kitchen has come back bigger and more refined after a fire consumed properties on the farm in 2017. The newly reconstructed bistro is a must-visit. The kitchen is one of the four exquisite restaurants on the Lanzerac Wine Estate. It boasts of the start of the art finishing, especially the fascinating chandeliers, and the best food and wines. Typically, breakfast is served in buffet style while lunches and dinners are made on order.

5. Overture located at Hidden Valley Wines

The Overture is a place for fine dining. It has gained a good reputation for serving authentic South African dishes. The fire-grilled plates are fascinating and delicious. The Overture never disappoints. Hidden Valley Wines that is located at the foot of Mountain Helderberg is one of the prolific wine farms Somerset West. The scenery is fantastic!

6. Delaire Graff Restaurant located at The Delaire Graff

A combination of fine dining, good scenery, elegant interiors and high-quality fermented grape juice in one of the best wine farms in Stellenbosch is the ultimate definition of luxury. You can expect nothing less than luxury at the Delaire Graff Restaurant. The meals are South African inspired, but with a special twist to make them more exotic. Asian cuisine is also served. The staff sources the ingredients used in food preparation from the farm and their wines are always flavourful.

7. Terroir located at Kleine Zalze Wine Estate

The Terroir Restaurant has been ranked as one of the top wine estates Stellenbosch because of its dynamism. The views across the rusty Oak trees are amazing. One can choose to dine inside the building or in the outdoors. The service is excellent, and the ambience is peaceful and relaxing. Although there are numerous wine estates in Cape Town, this one is outstanding because the proprietors and staff are dedicated to excellent service provision.

8. The Vine Bistro located at Glenelly

One of the finest wine farms Stellenbosch is the Glenelly Farm that has an equally amazing restaurant called The Vine Bistro. The restaurant is proud to be recognized in the list of best wine farms in Stellenbosch. The chefs often make adjustments to the menu depending on the season to break the monotony. The dishes served are mainly French cuisine. The perfect mix of 19th-century décor and contemporary designs make it a must-visit location for your next Stellenbosch wine tours.

9. Postcard Café located at Stark-Conde Wines

The list of the finest wine estates in Stellenbosch includes the Stark-Conde Wines that is home to the Postcard Café. The Café is laid back and is best for lazy but intimate gatherings with friends and family. The read has vast lawns that are well manicured, and there is a dam that adds to the peaceful ambience. The meals and the service are awesome, and all members of staff are friendly and welcoming.

10. The Tokara Restaurant located at Tokara Wine Estate

This exquisite restaurant is located at the edge of a cliff. From up there, the views are fantastic. One can see the vineyards that stretch across the valleys and hills. The ambience is peaceful and calm for the perfect bonding time with your loved ones. The meals are prepared with love, and the service is top-notch.

Visiting the Stellenbosch wine farms should be on every South African’s list. The farms have exquisite restaurants that serve the best meals to accompany the best fermented grape juices. Wine tasting, as well as other dynamic activities, are permitted on these farms. There is no better way to bond with your loved ones than fine dining over exquisite fermented grape juice and fantastic views. Plan your visit now!
