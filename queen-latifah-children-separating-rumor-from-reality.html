Who is Queen Latifah and who are Queen Latifah Children?

Queen Latifah, an American actress, and musician, was born on March 18th, 1970, in Newark, New Jersey, USA.

Her parents are Lancelot Amos Owens and Rita Lamae Bray, and she has an older brother, Lancelot Owens Jr.

Raised in the Baptist faith, Latifah attended Catholic schools in Newark, including Essex Catholic Girls’ High School, before graduating from Irvington High School.

After completing high school, she continued her education at Borough of Manhattan Community College.

At the age of eight, Latifah chose her stage name, which means “delicate” and “extremely kind” in Arabic.

She was always a tall individual, standing at 5-foot-10 inches (1.78 m), and even played power forward for her high school’s basketball team.

As a child, Latifah’s talent for singing was apparent, and she even performed in a production of the musical The Wiz, singing the song “Home.”

Despite her long career, she has kept her personal life private, leaving many to wonder if she has any children.

Does Queen Latifah have kids?

There have been rumors that Queen Latifah has a child, but neither she nor her partner has confirmed them.

So, to answer the question, no, Queen Latifah did not have a baby, but it is believed that she and her partner welcomed a son.

Who is Queen Latifah’s biological daughter? 

Regrettably, Queen Latifah does not have a daughter at present. 

However, some people have mistakenly assumed that Laya DeLeon Hayes is one of Queen Latifah’s children. 

This is not true, as Laya DeLeon Hayes was only Queen Latifah’s on-screen daughter in the critically acclaimed film Equalizer, and they do not have a biological relationship.

Is Queen Latifah married?

While there has been no news of a wedding for Queen Latifah, it is incorrect to assume that she is single. 

The actress is in a relationship with her long-time girlfriend, Eboni Nichols, whom she has been allegedly dating since 2013.

Eboni Nichols is an accomplished American actress, television personality, choreographer, and entrepreneur from Eagle, Ada County, Idaho. 

Apart from being Queen Latifah’s partner, she is renowned for her impressive talents as a dancer.
